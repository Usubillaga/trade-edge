The Math of Trading

Expectancy (E) is your mathematical edge. It calculates the average amount you will make or lose per trade over a series of 100+ trades.

If E is positive, you are operating a profitable business. If E is negative, you are gambling. You can have a 35% win rate and still be highly profitable if your Risk:Reward is 1:3 or higher.
